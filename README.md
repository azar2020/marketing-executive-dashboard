# marketing-executive-dashboard
Executive marketing dashboard analyzing channel performance, ROI, and full-funnel KPIs using Power BI.


# 📊 Marketing Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🎯 Objective

Build an executive-level dashboard to analyze marketing performance across channels, focusing on both **volume metrics (Revenue, Conversions)** and **efficiency metrics (ROAS, CAC)**.

---

## 📁 Dataset

Synthetic marketing dataset including:

* Impressions
* Clicks
* Conversions
* Spend
* Revenue
* Channel (Search, Email, Social, TV, Affiliate)

---

## 📈 Key Features

### 🔹 KPI Overview

* Revenue
* Marketing Spend
* Conversions
* CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
* ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
* CTR & Conversion Rate

---

### 🔹 Channel Performance

* Revenue by Channel
* ROAS by Channel
* Spend by Channel
* CAC by Channel

---

### 🔹 Trend Analysis

* Revenue vs Spend over time

---

### 🔹 Funnel Metrics

* Impressions → Clicks → Conversions

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Search drives the highest revenue but at moderate efficiency
* Email delivers the highest ROAS (most efficient channel)
* Conversion rate is relatively low, indicating funnel optimization opportunity
* Increased spend does not fully translate into proportional revenue growth

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* DAX (for calculated metrics like CAC, CTR, ROAS)

---

## 📌 Business Impact

This dashboard enables:

* Data-driven budget allocation
* Channel performance optimization
* Identification of funnel inefficiencies
